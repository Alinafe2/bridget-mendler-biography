Bridgit Mendler

Actress, Singer, STEM Advocate, and Role Model

Biography

Bridgit Mendler is a multifaceted talent excelling as an actress, singer, and academic. She rose to fame through her roles in Disney Channel productions and later distinguished herself in the STEM world, pursuing advanced studies in media and technology. Bridgit is a shining example of how creativity and science can intersect to create meaningful impact.

Education

  1. Bachelor’s Degree in Anthropology - University of Southern California
  2. Master’s Degree in Media Research - Harvard University
  3. PhD Candidate in Media and Technology - MIT Media Lab
